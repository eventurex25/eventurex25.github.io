About Tharangam 2025

Tharangam 2025 was EventureX's most ambitious cultural festival to date, transforming our campus into a vibrant celebration of creativity, talent, and community. Over three incredible days, we hosted more than 5,000 attendees from across the region, featuring an eclectic mix of performances, workshops, food vendors, and interactive experiences.

The festival kicked off with an opening ceremony that set the tone for the entire event - a spectacular light show synchronized with live music performances by both established and emerging artists. Our main stage featured headlining acts including indie rock bands, classical fusion ensembles, and contemporary dance groups, while multiple smaller venues hosted intimate acoustic sessions, poetry slams, and art installations.

What made Tharangam 2025 truly special was the seamless integration of student talent with professional entertainment. Our student performers shared the stage with renowned artists, creating a unique platform for emerging talent to shine. The festival also featured interactive workshops in music production, dance choreography, and visual arts, allowing attendees to learn from industry professionals.

Behind the scenes, our incredible crew of 120 students worked tirelessly to ensure every detail was perfect. From the technical wizards managing sound and lighting to the hospitality team ensuring everyone felt welcome, Tharangam 2025 was a testament to what students can achieve when passion meets professional execution.
