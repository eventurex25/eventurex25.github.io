Meet Our Founder

The visionary behind EventureX who believed in empowering students to create extraordinary events.

"We believe in empowering students, one event at a time."

Joseph Shibu

Founder

Joseph’s journey with EventureX began in his third year of engineering, where he is currently studying, when he noticed the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world experience in event management. Coming from a background in computer science with a passion for organizing college festivals, he witnessed firsthand how students had incredible creative potential but lacked structured opportunities to develop professional skills.

He founded EventureX with a clear mission: to provide college students with meaningful opportunities to earn money while gaining real-world experience in event management and related roles. As a student himself, he had often searched for part-time work and practical opportunities to develop professional skills but found very few structured options. This personal experience inspired him to create a platform specifically for students, where they could not only earn but also learn, grow, and gain exposure to the professional world.

Over time, Joseph’s initiative has resonated with students across multiple campuses, many of whom have approached him seeking part-time jobs, internships, and hands-on experience. Recognizing this demand, EventureX has evolved into a student-centric platform that bridges the gap between academic learning and practical application.

Under Joseph’s leadership, EventureX has successfully created an ecosystem where students can participate in events, manage projects, and explore various roles, all while developing critical skills such as leadership, teamwork, and time management. Beyond professional development, the platform empowers students to discover their unique potential, build confidence, and connect with like-minded peers.

Joseph remains committed to ensuring that every student who joins EventureX benefits both financially and professionally. He envisions a future where every college student has access to structured opportunities that enhance learning, foster creativity, and prepare them for the challenges of their careers—all while creating unforgettable experiences through student-led events.

The Story Behind EventureX

EventureX was born from a simple observation: while colleges across India hosted numerous events, students rarely had the opportunity to gain professional-level experience in event management. Joseph and Riya met during a particularly challenging college festival where they were both volunteering. Despite their different backgrounds – Joseph in technology and Riya in design – they shared a common frustration.

They noticed that most student "event committees" were more about hierarchy than learning, more about following instructions than developing skills. Students would spend hours on menial tasks without understanding the bigger picture or gaining transferable skills. Meanwhile, colleges were spending significant amounts on external event companies while their own students remained on the sidelines.

The turning point came when they successfully pitched an alternative approach to their college administration: let students lead, but provide them with proper structure, training, and mentorship. Their first event – a modest tech symposium – became a proof of concept. Not only did they deliver a successful event within budget, but the participating students gained skills in project management, client relations, technical production, and team leadership.

Word spread quickly. Other colleges began reaching out, students from different institutions wanted to join, and industry professionals started offering mentorship. What began as a college initiative evolved into EventureX – a platform that connects passionate students with meaningful opportunities in event management while delivering exceptional experiences for clients.

Today, EventureX operates on a simple but powerful premise: when you invest in students and provide them with real opportunities, they don't just meet expectations – they exceed them. Every event we create is a testament to what students can achieve when given the right platform, training, and trust.
