Meet Our Founders

The visionaries behind EventureX who believed in empowering students to create extraordinary events.

Arjun Mehta

Founder & Visionary

Arjun's journey with EventureX began during his third year of engineering when he noticed the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world experience in event management. Coming from a background in computer science with a passion for organizing college festivals, he witnessed firsthand how students had incredible creative potential but lacked structured opportunities to develop professional skills.

What started as a small initiative to organize his college's annual tech fest grew into something much bigger. Arjun realized that event management wasn't just about logistics ‚Äì it was about creating platforms where students could discover their talents, build confidence, and develop leadership skills. His vision was simple yet ambitious: create a student-centric organization that would bridge the gap between academic learning and industry experience.

Under his leadership, EventureX has grown from a college club to a recognized platform that has managed over 50 events and empowered more than 200 students. Arjun believes that every student has unique potential, and his role is to create environments where that potential can flourish. He's particularly passionate about sustainable event practices and integrating technology to enhance event experiences.

Today, as EventureX continues to expand, Arjun remains committed to the core mission: ensuring that every student who joins the team gains valuable skills, builds lasting connections, and contributes to creating unforgettable experiences for thousands of attendees.

Riya Sharma

Co-Founder & Creative Director

Riya's creative journey began in the world of graphic design and multimedia arts, but her true calling emerged when she started conceptualizing and designing experiences for college events. With a background in design thinking and a natural flair for understanding audience psychology, she became the creative force behind EventureX's most memorable experiences.

As Co-Founder and Creative Director, Riya oversees all aspects of creative development, from visual identity and stage design to content strategy and brand experiences. She believes that every event should tell a story, and her role is to ensure that story resonates with both participants and audiences. Her approach combines aesthetic excellence with functional design, ensuring that beautiful experiences are also practical and accessible.

Riya is particularly passionate about inclusive design and creating events that welcome people from all backgrounds. She has pioneered several initiatives within EventureX focused on accessibility, cultural sensitivity, and environmental responsibility. Her work has been instrumental in establishing EventureX's reputation for events that are not just well-executed, but thoughtfully crafted with attention to every detail.

Beyond her creative contributions, Riya is deeply committed to mentoring students interested in creative careers. She regularly conducts workshops on design thinking, creative problem-solving, and building portfolios. Her belief is that creativity isn't just about artistic skills ‚Äì it's about approaching challenges with curiosity, empathy, and the courage to try new approaches.

The Story Behind EventureX

EventureX was born from a simple observation: while colleges across India hosted numerous events, students rarely had the opportunity to gain professional-level experience in event management. Arjun and Riya met during a particularly challenging college festival where they were both volunteering. Despite their different backgrounds ‚Äì Arjun in technology and Riya in design ‚Äì they shared a common frustration.

They noticed that most student "event committees" were more about hierarchy than learning, more about following instructions than developing skills. Students would spend hours on menial tasks without understanding the bigger picture or gaining transferable skills. Meanwhile, colleges were spending significant amounts on external event companies while their own students remained on the sidelines.

The turning point came when they successfully pitched an alternative approach to their college administration: let students lead, but provide them with proper structure, training, and mentorship. Their first event ‚Äì a modest tech symposium ‚Äì became a proof of concept. Not only did they deliver a successful event within budget, but the participating students gained skills in project management, client relations, technical production, and team leadership.

Word spread quickly. Other colleges began reaching out, students from different institutions wanted to join, and industry professionals started offering mentorship. What began as a college initiative evolved into EventureX ‚Äì a platform that connects passionate students with meaningful opportunities in event management while delivering exceptional experiences for clients.

Today, EventureX operates on a simple but powerful premise: when you invest in students and provide them with real opportunities, they don't just meet expectations ‚Äì they exceed them. Every event we create is a testament to what students can achieve when given the right platform, training, and trust.
